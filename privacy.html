Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Who We Are

Sprint People Solutions (“Sprint”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) is a recruitment and fractional HR advisory firm based in Mumbai, India. We operate this website at sprintpeople.in and any associated sub-pages.

For any privacy-related queries, please contact us at shreya@sprintpeople.in.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect personal data only when you voluntarily provide it through our contact form. This may include:

  • Full name
  • Company name
  • Work email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • The nature of your enquiry
  • Any additional information you choose to share in the message field

We do not collect data passively through tracking pixels, third-party analytics, or advertising tags on this website.

3. How We Use Your Data

The information you submit is used solely to:

  • Respond to your enquiry
  • Assess whether our services are a fit for your needs
  • Communicate with you about next steps, if relevant

We will never use your contact details for unsolicited marketing or share them with third parties for commercial purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

Our contact form is processed by Formspree (formspree.io), a form handling service. When you submit the form, your data is transmitted to Formspree’s servers and then forwarded to us by email. Formspree’s privacy policy governs how they handle this data in transit.

We may also use Google Workspace for internal email and document storage. Data shared with us by email is subject to Google’s data processing terms.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party.

5. Data Retention

We retain your contact details only for as long as is necessary to fulfil the purpose for which they were collected — typically the duration of an active client conversation or hiring engagement. Enquiries that do not result in an engagement are removed from our records within 12 months.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data at any time
  • Withdraw consent for us to contact you

To exercise any of these rights, email us at shreya@sprintpeople.in. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Cookies

This website does not use cookies, local storage, or any client-side tracking mechanism. No data is stored on your device when you visit this site.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page indicates when the most recent changes were made. Continued use of the website after any changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
